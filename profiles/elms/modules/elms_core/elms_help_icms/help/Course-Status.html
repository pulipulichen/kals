The course status page will tell you about the relative "health" of the course you are viewing.  To access Course Status, go to the front-facing page of a course.  Then click on the Course Status tab that appears on the page.  This will display statistics about the course like:

  • How many Versions / Offerings are associated to this course
  • Pieces of content associated to this course
  • Settings specific to the configuration of the course (like name)
